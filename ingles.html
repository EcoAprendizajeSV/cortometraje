"Under the Shadow of Influence"

The short film narrates the life of Charlie, a brilliant student who has always stood out for his dedication and academic effort. Since he was a child, his dreams were big, but everything changes when he enters adolescence and joins a group of friends in high school: Jorge and Eduardo. Although at first the friendship seems harmless, it soon turns into negative pressure when they invite him to explore a nearby pond, a place that becomes the stage for his transformation.

Moral

The story concludes with a reflection based on Proverbs 22:6: "Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it." The young people understand that true influence comes from those who inspire them to be the best version of themselves. They decide that they will not let peer pressure divert them from their dreams, always choosing the path that brings them closer to their goals.
